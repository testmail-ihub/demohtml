CIO Morning Briefing

Voice-first executive command surface on Amazon Echo Show 5 — powered by ServiceNow + Now Assist
🔥

The Problem

CIOs start every morning digging through dashboards, emails, and Slack to piece together "what happened overnight." By the time they have context, the first meeting has already started.

💡

The Solution

One voice command — "Alexa, morning briefing" — delivers a 60-second spoken + visual summary with P1s, changes, approvals, health, and an AI-generated risk insight. Act by voice.

🎯

The Demo

Live Echo Show 5 connected to a ServiceNow instance. Trigger the briefing, watch 6 screens auto-play, approve a change by voice, and hear Now Assist flag a change/incident conflict.
